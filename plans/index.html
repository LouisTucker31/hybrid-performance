FAQs

Common questions about the plans.

What's included in each training plan?

Every plan comes with a PDF guide and a spreadsheet tracker, covering weekly structure, session breakdowns, progression and substitutions, plus notes on managing fatigue and recovery.

Do I need a gym membership to follow these plans?

A gym or a well-equipped home setup works. Each plan notes what equipment the sessions assume, and substitutions are included if something isn't available to you.

Are these plans suitable for beginners?

Yes, plans are labelled by level (Beginner, Triathlon, Endurance) so you can pick the one that matches where you're starting from.

How do I get the plan after I buy it?

Plans are sold through Gumroad and delivered instantly as a digital download, the PDF guide and spreadsheet tracker land in your inbox as soon as payment goes through.

Is there support included, or am I following it alone?

These are self-guided plans you follow independently. If you'd prefer ongoing input or something built around you specifically, a Bespoke Plan is the better fit.

Is there a triathlon training plan?

Yes, the First Sprint Triathlon plan is built for exactly that, with a Century Ride Blueprint for cyclists coming soon.
