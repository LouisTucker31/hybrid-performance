FAQs

Common questions about this plan.

How is this different from a generic triathlon plan?

This plan is built specifically for a first sprint triathlon, three sessions a week rather than the higher volume of a generic triathlon plan, with a taper built in for race week.

Do I need to already be a strong swimmer?

No. The swim sessions progress from wherever you're starting, confidence in the water is built gradually alongside the bike and run.

Will three sessions a week be enough to finish?

Yes, for a first sprint distance, three well-placed sessions a week across swim, bike and run is enough to arrive prepared and finish strong.

What if I don't have easy access to a pool, bike or open water?

The plan notes sensible substitutions where equipment or access is limited, so a missing pool or bike doesn't derail the week.

What do I need to use the tracker?

Just a free Google account for Google Sheets, or Microsoft Excel if you prefer. Both versions are included.

Is there support if I get stuck?

This is a self-guided plan without ongoing coaching support. If you'd prefer input along the way, a Bespoke Plan is the better fit.
